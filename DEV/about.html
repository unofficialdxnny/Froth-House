The Experience

Step into a world of sensory delights and late-night relaxation.

A Sanctuary for the
Senses

The Midnight Oasis was conceived as a getaway from the bustling city streets, bringing the rich traditions of a Moroccan tea house directly to you. From the moment you walk in, you are greeted by the warm glow of traditional mosaic lamps and the subtle aroma of premium shisha.

We pride ourselves on using only the highest grade tobacco and coconut coals. Our "shisha sommeliers" are always on hand to help you craft the perfect flavor mix, while our kitchen prepares authentic Moroccan mint tea and traditional treats like Baklava.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just needing a place to decompress, experience true Moroccan hospitality with us.
